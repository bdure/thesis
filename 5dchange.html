Changing with technology

When asked how technology has affected their work, most participants expressed excitement over the research opportunities. Other responses included:
  • Web B: "We are all technology. It has created my job, and I think it's terrific."
  • Newspaper A: "We have to keep our eye on many more potential news sources than years ago mostly Internet sources and cable broadcast sources."
  • TV A: "Technology is just a tool. You just have to be smart with how you use it, instead of letting it control you."
  • TV B: "The avalanche of technology has made me focus more on local, local, local."
