Music: You could describe my taste as "alternative" ... except that alternative music is awful now. Where a few years ago we had Belly and XTC, we now have Third Eye Blind and Matchbox 20. We have fifth-rate Pearl Jams and fourth-rate Hooties. Still, I buy a few CDs, and when I find something I like, I try to pass it on.
